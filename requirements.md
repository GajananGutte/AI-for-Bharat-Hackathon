# Requirements Document  
## Project Title: PublicAI – AI-powered Civic Assistance Platform



## 1. Introduction  
PublicAI is an AI-powered public assistance platform designed to simplify civic complaint resolution and improve access to government information through multilingual and voice-enabled support.



## 2. Problem Overview  
Citizens, especially in rural areas and senior citizens, face difficulties in reporting civic issues and understanding government forms due to complex systems and language barriers.



## 3. Objectives  
- Simplify complaint submission  
- Enable AI-based complaint classification  
- Provide automatic department routing  
- Offer multilingual & voice support  
- Ensure real-time status tracking  



## 4. Functional Requirements  

### 4.1 Complaint Management  
- Users can submit complaints via text, voice, or image  
- System classifies complaint using AI  
- Complaint routed to appropriate department  
- Unique complaint ID generated  
- Status tracking available  

### 4.2 Language Assistance  
- Upload government forms/notices  
- AI translates content  
- Simplified explanations provided  
- Voice playback support  

### 4.3 Notifications  
- Real-time complaint updates  
- Alerts & reminders  



## 5. Non-Functional Requirements  
- Scalability (Cloud-based AWS)  
- Reliability  
- Low latency  
- Security of user data  
- User-friendly interface  



## 6. Target Users  
- General citizens  
- Rural communities  
- Senior citizens  
- Municipal/Government authorities  



## 7. Constraints  
- Limited development time  
- Budget constraints (student project)  



## 8. Expected Outcome  
An AI-driven prototype demonstrating automated complaint resolution, multilingual accessibility, and transparent public service delivery.
